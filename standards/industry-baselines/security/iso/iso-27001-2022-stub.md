## Publicly-known structure

ISO/IEC 27001:2022 specifies requirements for an Information Security Management System (ISMS). The standard contains:

- **Clauses 4–10**: ISMS requirements (context, leadership, planning, support, operation, performance evaluation, improvement)
- **Annex A**: 93 controls organized into 4 themes (down from 14 categories in the 2013 edition):
  - **A.5 Organizational controls** (37 controls)
  - **A.6 People controls** (8 controls)
  - **A.7 Physical controls** (14 controls)
  - **A.8 Technological controls** (34 controls)
