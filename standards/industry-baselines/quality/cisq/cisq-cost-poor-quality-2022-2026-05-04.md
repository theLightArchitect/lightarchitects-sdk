## About CISQ

The **Consortium for Information & Software Quality (CISQ)** is the trusted leader in advancing measurable standards to enhance software quality, reliability, and security. By addressing critical challenges such as software weaknesses, cybersecurity vulnerabilities, and technical debt, CISQ empowers organizations to deliver stronger, more resilient digital solutions.

CISQ was co-founded by the [Object Management Group® (OMG®)](https://www.omg.org/) and [Software Engineering Institute (SEI)](https://www.sei.cmu.edu/) at Carnegie Mellon University.

### Mission

CISQ establishes globally recognized standards and best practices that enable organizations to assess, manage, and improve the structural integrity of their software. These standards help identify and mitigate risks early in the development lifecycle, saving costs and ensuring high-quality deliverables.
